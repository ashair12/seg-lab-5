ABOUT US

Hillside Physiotherapy is a small physiotherapy clinic located in Toronto. The clinic opened in 2014 and has served many clients with a wide variety of services. We offer high quality medical care and services for all of your physiotherapy needs, whether it be sore muscles, rehabilitation, or knee pain, we can help.

Services


Orthotics

Get custom orthotic in-soles for your shoes. Improve foot balance and stay comfortable while walking, running, or jumping.

Price: $300-$500

Rehabilitation

Recently suffered a ligament tear or a broken bone? Book an appointment for rehabilitation to get you back on your feet.

Price: $400

Custom Knee Brace

Get a custom knee brace built just for your knees. This brace will aid people with knee pain as well as people rehabilitating from knee injuries.

Price: $600

Chiropractic

See a professional chriopractor to diagnose, treat, and prevent issues that are affecting your musculoskeletal system.

Price: $400

Sports Therapy

Book an appointment for sports therapy if you are constantly affected by sports-related injuries. Our experts will help you fix your biomechanics.

Price: $250

Massage Therapy

Price: $200
